A Good Program

发表于 2019-09-26

A Good Program should:

  1. Give the correct answer.
  2. Be clear and easy to read, with the action of each part easy to analyse.
  3. Document itself for the sake of readers and the programmer.
  4. Be easy to use.
  5. Be built up out of small programs that can be independently varified.
  6. Be easy to modify and robust enough to keep giving correct answer after modification and simple debugging.
  7. Document the data formats used.
  8. Use trusted libraries.
  9. Be published or passed on to others to use and to develop further.
